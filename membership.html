Income Dependent Pricing

The IDP programme is H/Art Academy's commitment to making Jiu Jitsu accessible to anyone in Cambodia. Individuals who genuinely face budget limitations but wish to train, can come speak to us directly at the Academy in order to find a workable solution. A portion of the regular fees paid by members is used to subsidise fees for Cambodian individuals who cannot afford to pay full membership fees.
